Grillmix

4 servings 75 minutes Save Recipe

A smoky and flavorful grill mix with juicy meat pieces, grilled vegetables, and a spicy marinade. Perfect for a summer evening by the grill.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast (or mixed meat)
  • 4 potatoes
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, black pepper, paprika powder, garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken breast, potatoes, zucchini, bell pepper, and red onion into chunks.
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl with olive oil and spices.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the ingredients onto skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for 10-15 minutes, turning them regularly until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve with a fresh salad or rice.
