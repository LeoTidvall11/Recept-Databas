French Toast with Blueberries and Syrup

4 servings 30 minutes Save Recipe

French Toast is a classic breakfast or brunch dish that is both simple and delicious. It consists of bread dipped in an egg and milk mixture, pan-fried until golden brown and served with various accompaniments such as jam, fresh berries or syrup. It is a perfect dish to start your day with a tasty treat!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (preferably a bit thicker)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 dl milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Jam, berries or syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla sugar, and sugar in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring it is well-coated but not soggy.
  3. Melt butter or heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Fry the bread slices for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Serve immediately with jam, fresh berries, or syrup to taste.
