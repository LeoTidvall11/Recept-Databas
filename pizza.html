Pizza

4 servings 30 minutes Save Recipe

A creamy chicken pizza topped with juicy pineapple, red onion, and a tangy sauce. Perfect balance of sweet and savory in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 100g tomato sauce
  • 150g grated cheese
  • 50g red onion
  • 100g chicken
  • 100g ham
  • 50g pineapple

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the grated cheese over the sauce.
  5. Place the chicken and red onion on top.
  6. Place the ham and pineapple on top.
  7. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the cheese is golden brown.
